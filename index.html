Frequently asked questions

What is the Goethe-Zertifikat A1?

The Goethe-Zertifikat A1 is an internationally recognized German language exam for beginners. It proves you can understand and use basic everyday expressions — enough for simple conversations about yourself, your family, and immediate needs. It's commonly required for family reunification visas and basic residence permits in Germany.

How long is the Goethe A1 exam?

The written portion (reading, listening, writing) takes about 65 minutes. The speaking exam is an additional 15 minutes, conducted in a small group. Total exam time is approximately 80 minutes.

What score do I need to pass?

You need at least 60 out of 100 points to pass. The written module (Lesen, Hören, Schreiben) and the speaking module (Sprechen) are each scored separately, and you must pass both modules.

How does the AI writing review work?

After you write your response, the AI instantly evaluates your grammar, vocabulary, and task completion against A1 criteria. It highlights specific errors, suggests corrections, and gives you a score estimate — helping you improve without waiting for a teacher.
